# GoSH Website

GoSH is a travel platform specifically designed for tourists seeking to explore Shanghai but who may feel uncertain about where to start.

The website aims to help users discover top attractions and local dining options with ease. Besides, it shows the beauty and rich culture of Shanghai to a broader audience.

---

# User guidence:

## 1. Login Page

- Users are required to enter a "username" and "password" (must not be empty).
- Clicking the "Log In" button will redirect users to the homepage.
- The "Reset Password" and "Register" links are placeholders for future development.

---

## 2. Home Page

- The "Home" text in the top navigation bar will enlarge and change color to indicate that the user is currently on the homepage.
- Three interactive cards are displayed. Clicking on a card will navigate the user to the specific section of the website.

---

## 3. Dining Page

- The "Dining" text in the navigation bar becomes larger and changes color to indicate the current page.
- A dropdown menu at the top allows users to filter restaurants by category.
- Hovering over a restaurant card will show a short introduction.
- Clicking the restaurant's name or the Google Maps icon opens the restaurant’s location and details on Google Maps.
- Clicking the "star" icon at the top-right corner of the card adds the restaurant to favorites.
- Clicking the left/right arrow icons turns the page to the last/next one.

---

## 4. Sights Page

- The "Sights" text in the navigation bar becomes larger and changes color to indicate the current page.
- Hovering over a sight card shows a short introduction.
- Clicking the sight's name or the Google Maps icon opens the location and details on Google Maps.
- Clicking the "star" icon at the top-right corner of the card adds the sight to favorites.
- The arrow icons on the left or right side of each row allow horizontal scrolling of cards.

---

## 5. About Page

- The "About" text in the navigation bar becomes larger and changes color to indicate the current page.
- This page explains the website’s advantages.
- A contact form is provided here for users to get in touch with the developer.

---

## 6. Me Page

- This is the personal page where users can view their favorited restaurants and sights.
- Due to technical and time constraints, some interactive features are currently missing and will be added in the future.
- Clicking the "Logout" button logs the user out and returns to the login page.

---

# Responsive Design:

The website supports responsive design for mobile and tablet screens to ensure optimal user experience.

Target devices:

- iPhone XR (414\*896)
- iPhone 14 Pro MAX (430\*932)
- iPad Pro (1366\*1024) **(landscape orientation only)**
- iPad Air (1180\*820) **(landscape orientation only)**
